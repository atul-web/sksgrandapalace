WELCOME TO SKS GRAND PALACE, THE BEST HOTELIN VRINDAVAN

Hotel SKS Grand Palace, a new hotel in Vrindavan, has opened its doors and is ready to host the most lavish events and ceremonies for you and your loved ones. This hotel in Vrindavan showcases marvellous amenities alongside spacious and luxurious venues, making it the perfect choice for your stay in Mathura and Vrindavan. The hotel is centrally located between Mathura and Vrindavan, with close proximity to all the major temples and tourist attractions of Mathura district, including Banke Bihari temple, Prem Mandir, Dwarkadhish temple, Krishna Janam Bhumi, Barsana, Gokul, Govardhan, Raman Reti, and Tatiya Sthan.
